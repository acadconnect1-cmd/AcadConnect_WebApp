Senior Professor of Quantum Physics

Full-time Tenure Track $180k - $240k

Role Overview

Stanford University's Department of Physics is seeking an eminent scholar for the position of Senior Professor of Quantum Physics. This tenure-track role offers a unique opportunity to lead transformative research at the intersection of quantum mechanics, information theory, and advanced material sciences.

The successful candidate will be expected to maintain an internationally recognized research program, mentor doctoral students, and contribute to our undergraduate curriculum with innovative teaching methods.

Qualifications

  • check_circle Ph.D. in Physics or closely related field.
  • check_circle Minimum 10 years of post-doctoral research experience.
  • check_circle Proven track record of high-impact publications.
  • check_circle Experience in securing large-scale research funding.

Institutional Culture

At Stanford, we believe that academic diversity is the cornerstone of discovery.
